🎭 Voice Options: Professional vs Cloning

Option A: Professional Voice

Use one of ElevenLabs' pre-made professional voices

✅ Pros
  • Broadcast-ready quality immediately
  • No recording needed
  • Consistent output
  • Lower cost ($11/mo Creator plan)
  • Instant setup
  • Wide variety to choose from
❌ Cons
  • Not unique to your brand
  • Others may use same voice
  • Less personal connection
Recommended $11/month

Option B: Professional Voice Cloning (PVC)

Clone Jayna's voice with professional quality

✅ Pros
  • Unique to your brand
  • Personal connection with audience
  • Can monetize the voice later
  • Most authentic option
❌ Cons
  • Requires 30+ min clean audio
  • Higher cost ($99/mo Pro plan)
  • Longer setup time
  • Quality depends on source audio
  • Jayna must review every episode
Future Option $99/month

Option C: Instant Voice Cloning (IVC)

Quick clone with just a few minutes of audio

✅ Pros
  • Fastest setup (minutes)
  • Only needs 1-5 min audio
  • Included in Creator plan
❌ Cons
  • Lower quality than PVC
  • Less consistent
  • Not suitable for professional podcast
  • May sound robotic
Not Recommended Included in $11/mo
