🎬 Differentiation Strategy for ADHD Niche

Unique Angle: "ADHD for Inconsistent Overachievers"

Position as the podcast for high-functioning ADHDers who struggle with consistency despite being capable. This differentiates from:

  • Medical/clinical podcasts (too dry, focused on diagnosis)
  • Parenting ADHD podcasts (focused on kids, not adults)
  • Productivity podcasts (don't understand ADHD-specific challenges)

Content Format Innovations

  • "2-Minute Tools": Ultra-short, actionable episodes for ADHD attention spans
  • "The 5-Minute Reset": Guided micro-interventions listeners can do immediately
  • "ADHD Reality Check": Honest conversations about what actually works vs. what should work
  • "Success Stories (That Aren't Annoying)": Realistic wins from real people, not guru perfection
  • "The Anti-Planner": Systems for people who hate traditional planning methods

Distribution Hacks

  • Reddit Strategy: r/ADHD (1.8M members), r/adhdwomen (600k+), r/ADHD_Programmers - provide value before promoting
  • TikTok/Instagram: 30-60 second clips of "ADHD things you thought were just you" (highly viral format)
  • Newsletter: "The Monday Reset" - one actionable tip per week (low commitment)
  • Community: Private Discord for listeners (ADHDers love community)
  • Cross-promotion: Partner with ADHD coaches, therapists, app creators

📅 90-Day Launch Strategy

Month 1: Foundation

  • Record 10 episodes (bank content before launch)
  • Set up podcast hosting (Buzzsprout/Anchor)
  • Create social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X)
  • Build simple landing page with email capture
  • Join ADHD communities and provide value

Month 2: Launch & Learn

  • Launch with 5 episodes (give listeners immediate value)
  • Post 1-2 short videos daily on TikTok/Instagram
  • Engage in Reddit communities daily
  • Reach out to 20 ADHD creators for cross-promotion
  • Launch weekly newsletter

Month 3: Scale

  • Analyze which episodes perform best, double down
  • Start guest interviews (ADHD experts, successful ADHDers)
  • Launch first digital product (simple ADHD planner)
  • Apply for podcast advertising networks
  • Consider paid ads on Meta (targeting ADHD interests)
