## RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### Risk 1: Platform Algorithm Changes
**Impact:** High — could crater traffic overnight
**Mitigation:** 
- Diversify across 3+ platforms (YouTube, podcast platforms, audiobook stores)
- Build email list from day one (owned audience)
- Never rely on single platform

### Risk 2: AI Content Detection/Restrictions
**Impact:** Medium — platforms may throttle AI content
**Mitigation:**
- Human-in-the-loop review (Jayna approves all content)
- Focus on high-quality, valuable content (not spam)
- Adapt quickly if policies change
- Mix AI with curated human research

### Risk 3: Niche Saturation
**Impact:** Medium — too many competitors
**Mitigation:**
- Target micro-niches ("ADHD productivity for moms" not "productivity")
- Move fast on trending topics
- Build content moats (comprehensive guides, series)
- Continuous testing of new niches
