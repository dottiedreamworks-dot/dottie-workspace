🆕 NEW: Multi-City Routing Options

User Question: "Were there no viable options to fly into Asia somewhere else cheaper, then go to Korea, or skip Korea entirely?"

Here are the alternative routing scenarios, including flying into other Asian hubs first, then connecting to Seoul:

Option A: Nashville → Tokyo → Seoul MULTI-CITY
BNA → NRT/HND → ICN → BNA
Total: ~$1,050 - $1,650

Breakdown: Nashville-Tokyo (~$900-1,400) + Tokyo-Seoul (~$150-250)

✅ Pros

  • Experience Japan + Korea in one trip
  • Tokyo-Seoul flight is short (~2.5 hrs)
  • Many flight options between cities
  • Japan is extremely family-friendly

❌ Cons

  • More complex itinerary
  • Japan is expensive (hotels, food)
  • Need to coordinate 2 destinations
  • Similar cost to direct Seoul
Option B: Nashville → Taipei → Seoul MULTI-CITY
BNA → TPE → ICN → BNA
Total: ~$1,110 - $1,580

Breakdown: Nashville-Taipei (~$950-1,300) + Taipei-Seoul (~$160-280)

✅ Pros

  • Taiwan is affordable and safe
  • Great food and night markets
  • English signage common in Taipei
  • Short flight to Seoul (~2.5 hrs)

❌ Cons

  • More expensive than direct Seoul
  • Hot and humid in June
  • Typhoon season starts
  • Less "wow factor" than Japan
Option C: Nashville → Philippines → Seoul MULTI-CITY
BNA → MNL → ICN → BNA
Total: ~$983 - $1,400

Breakdown: Nashville-Manila (~$850-1,200) + Manila-Seoul (~$133-200)

✅ Pros

  • Cheapest multi-city option
  • Beaches + city combo
  • English widely spoken
  • Very affordable once there

❌ Cons

  • Manila airport can be chaotic
  • Hot and humid in June
  • Monsoon season
  • Need domestic flights for beaches

🎯 Updated Recommendations

If You Want to Include Korea:

Best Value: Seoul + Philippines — Still the winner at $1,148-1,700 total. The Philippines add-on is cheap ($133-200) and gives you beaches after Seoul.


Best Experience: Seoul + Japan — For ~$100-200 more, you get two incredible countries. Japan is magical for kids and the flight to Seoul is only 2.5 hours.

If You're Open to Skipping Korea:

Easiest: Meet in Hawaii — No passports needed, everyone speaks English, beaches are perfect for kids. Total cost similar to Seoul trip ($1,234-1,600).


Most Memorable: Meet in Japan — Unique cultural experience, extremely safe, husband has short flight. Total cost $1,050-1,700.

💡 Key Insight

Multi-city routing (Nashville → Asia → Seoul) doesn't save money compared to direct Seoul flights. The real savings come from:

  • Meeting in Hawaii (saves ~$200-400 vs Seoul)
  • Adding Philippines to Seoul (only $133-200 extra)
  • Meeting in Japan (similar cost, better experience)

💡 Pro tip: If your husband can get time off, meeting in Japan or Hawaii gives you the best family experience for similar cost to Seoul-only.
