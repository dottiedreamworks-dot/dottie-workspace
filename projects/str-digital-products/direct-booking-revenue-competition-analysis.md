# Direct Booking & Revenue Management - Competitive Analysis
**Date:** March 8, 2026  
**Focus:** Courses, training programs, and educational resources

---

## Executive Summary

The direct booking and revenue management education market has a **massive price gap** between free content and premium courses ($500-$7,000+). There's a clear opportunity for **mid-tier priced resources** ($50-$200) that offer more depth than free content but don't require the investment of full coaching programs.

**Key Finding:** The market is dominated by either:
- Free blog posts/podcasts (surface-level)
- High-ticket courses ($500-$7,000) with coaching
- **Gap in the middle:** Comprehensive, actionable resources under $200
