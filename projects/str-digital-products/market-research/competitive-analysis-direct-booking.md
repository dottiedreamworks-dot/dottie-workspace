## 5. Identified Gaps & Opportunities

### Gap #1: Focused Direct Booking System
**Current State:** Direct bookings covered as one module in comprehensive courses
**Opportunity:** Standalone product focused ONLY on direct booking growth
**Target:** Owners who already have properties, want to reduce OTA dependence

### Gap #2: Implementation-Ready Templates
**Current State:** Generic templates that require heavy customization
**Opportunity:** Pre-customized templates with setup instructions for specific platforms (OwnerRez, Lodgify, etc.)
**Target:** Time-constrained owners who want plug-and-play solutions

### Gap #3: Email Automation Workflows
**Current State:** Individual email templates or software with basic automation
**Opportunity:** Complete email sequence workflows with copy, timing, and trigger instructions
**Target:** Owners using any PMS or email platform

### Gap #4: Affordable Middle Tier
**Current State:** Gap between $50 templates and $500+ courses
**Opportunity:** Comprehensive system at $150-300 price point
**Target:** Serious owners not ready for high-ticket coaching

### Gap #5: Maintenance + Direct Booking Bundle
**Current State:** No combined offering
**Opportunity:** System that covers both property maintenance AND direct booking growth
**Target:** Professional hosts and small property managers

## 8. Key Insights

1. **Market is crowded with courses** but light on implementation-ready systems
2. **High-ticket coaching** ($1,000+) exists but leaves gap at $200-500 range
3. **Software solutions** provide tools but not strategy
4. **Template market** is fragmented, no comprehensive systems
5. **Maintenance + marketing combo** is completely untapped
6. **Email automation** is mentioned everywhere but rarely taught in depth
