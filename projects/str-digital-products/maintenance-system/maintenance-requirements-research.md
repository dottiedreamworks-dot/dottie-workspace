**Research Date:** March 8, 2026

**Status:** Completed

## Executive Summary

This research document compiles comprehensive maintenance requirements for short-term rental (STR) properties. The data is organized by frequency (weekly, monthly, seasonal, annual) and category (systems, safety, appliances, exterior, compliance) to support the development of a maintenance tracking system.

---

## 1. HVAC Systems

### Filters
- **Frequency:** Change every 1-3 months (monthly if pets allowed)
- **Task:** Replace air filters in HVAC systems; clean filters of floor/window units
- **Cost Impact:** Regular changes improve efficiency and prevent costly repairs

### Professional Servicing
- **Spring:** Service AC unit before hot season (March-April)
- **Fall:** Service furnace/heating system before cold season (September-October)
- **Annual:** Full professional HVAC inspection and tune-up
- **Cost:** $100-300 per service visit

### Temperature Monitoring
- Monitor temperature/humidity to detect system failures early
- Integrate with smart sensors for remote monitoring

---

## 2. Safety Systems

### Smoke & Carbon Monoxide Detectors
- **Monthly:** Test all detectors
- **Bi-annual:** Replace batteries (or when low)
- **7-year replacement:** Replace entire units every 7 years
- **Regulatory:** Required by law in all STR properties

### Fire Extinguishers
- **Monthly:** Verify accessible, charged, and in good condition
- **Annual:** Professional inspection/recharge
- **Placement:** Kitchen, near bedrooms, common areas

### Locks & Security
- **Weekly:** Verify all door and window locks function properly
- **Monthly:** Replace smart lock batteries as needed
- **Quarterly:** Lubricate hinges and mechanisms

### Emergency Equipment
- **Winter:** Stock emergency kit (flashlights, batteries, first aid)
- **Annual:** Review and refresh emergency supplies

## 3. Appliances

### Major Appliance Lifespans & Maintenance

| Appliance | Lifespan | Maintenance Schedule |
|-----------|----------|---------------------|
| Refrigerator | 10-15 years | Coil cleaning every 6 months; annual professional service |
| Range/Oven | 10-15 years | Annual professional inspection; clean after heavy use |
| Dishwasher | 7-10 years | Monthly filter cleaning; annual professional service |
| Washing Machine | 8-12 years | Monthly drum cleaning; check hoses every 6 months |
| Dryer | 8-12 years | **Clean lint trap after every load**; annual vent cleaning |
| Microwave | 8-10 years | Regular interior cleaning; check door seals |

### Appliance Maintenance Tasks
- **Weekly:** Quick function test of all major appliances
- **Monthly:** Clean filters, check for leaks, inspect hoses
- **Annual:** Professional cleaning and inspection for all major appliances

---

## 4. Plumbing Systems

### Regular Checks
- **Weekly:** Inspect under sinks and around toilets for leaks
- **Monthly:** Pour drain cleaner/baking soda solution down all drains
- **Annual:** Professional plumbing system inspection

### Water Heater
- **Annual:** Flush tank, check pressure relief valve
- **Lifespan:** 8-12 years (plan for replacement)

### Winterization (Cold Climates)
- **Fall:** Disconnect outdoor hoses, drain sprinkler systems
- **Winter:** Check pipe insulation; monitor for freezing
- **Emergency:** Plan for pipe thawing if frozen

---

## 5. Exterior Maintenance

### Roof & Gutters
- **Spring & Fall:** Clean gutters and downspouts
- **Annual:** Professional roof inspection
- **After storms:** Visual inspection from ground for damage

### Decks & Patios
- **Spring:** Inspect for loose boards, splinters, safety hazards
- **Summer:** Clean and repair outdoor furniture
- **As needed:** Power wash, reseal/stain (every 2-3 years)

### Landscaping
- **Weekly:** Basic curb appeal check; clear pathways
- **Spring:** Pruning, planting, winter damage repair
- **Summer:** Regular mowing, watering, pest control
- **Fall:** Leaf removal, winterization prep
- **Winter:** Snow/ice removal planning and execution

### Exterior Paint & Siding
- **Annual:** Inspect for wear, damage, peeling
- **Every 3-5 years:** Touch-up or full repaint as needed

---

## 6. Interior Maintenance

### Deep Cleaning Schedule
- **Between guests:** Standard turnover cleaning
- **Monthly:** Detailed inspection of furniture, bedding, decor
- **Quarterly:** Deep clean appliances, windows, carpets
- **Annual:** Professional carpet cleaning, floor resealing

### Furniture & Decor
- **Weekly:** Check for damage or instability
- **Monthly:** Assess condition of upholstery, decorative items
- **Mattresses:** Rotate every 6 months; replace every 5-7 years

### Windows & Doors
- **Spring:** Deep clean interior and exterior
- **Monthly:** Check for drafts, damaged screens, proper locks
- **Fall:** Check weather stripping and seals

---

## 7. Pools, Hot Tubs & Recreation Equipment

### Pools
- **Weekly:** Chemical testing and adjustment
- **Monthly:** Equipment check, cleaning
- **Seasonal:** Opening/closing procedures
- **Professional:** Weekly service recommended

### Hot Tubs
- **Weekly:** Chemical levels, filter check
- **Monthly:** Deep clean, filter replacement
- **Professional:** Regular service for complex issues

### Other Amenities
- **Gym equipment:** Monthly safety and function check
- **Bicycles:** Quarterly tune-up and safety check
- **BBQ/Grill:** Clean after each guest use; deep clean monthly

---

## 8. Regulatory Compliance

### Permits & Licenses
- **Annual renewal:** STR permits (varies by jurisdiction)
- **Fees:** $100-500+ annually depending on location

### Inspections
- **Fire Life Safety Inspection:** Required every 3 years in many jurisdictions
- **Defensible Space Inspection:** Required every 3 years (fire-prone areas)
- **Health inspections:** Some locations require periodic checks
- **Certificate of Occupancy:** Verify current and valid

### Insurance
- **Annual review:** Ensure adequate coverage for STR use
- **Requirements:** Many jurisdictions require STR-specific insurance

### Tax Compliance
- **Monthly/Quarterly:** Transient occupancy tax collection and remittance
- **Annual:** Income reporting, property tax assessments

---

## 9. Technology & Smart Home Systems

### Smart Locks
- **Monthly:** Battery check/replacement
- **Quarterly:** Software updates, access code rotation

### WiFi & Entertainment
- **Monthly:** Speed test, equipment check
- **Quarterly:** Router restart, password updates if needed

### Security Systems
- **Monthly:** Test alarms, cameras, sensors
- **Annual:** Professional inspection and update

### Remote Monitoring
- **Noise monitoring:** Battery check, calibration
- **Climate monitoring:** Sensor battery and function check

---

## 10. Vendor & Contractor Management

### Regular Service Providers
- **Cleaning crew:** Between every guest turnover
- **Landscaping:** Weekly/bi-weekly during growing season
- **Pool service:** Weekly (if applicable)
- **Pest control:** Quarterly or as needed

### On-Call Contractors
- **Plumber:** For emergencies and annual inspections
- **Electrician:** For issues and annual inspections
- **HVAC technician:** Seasonal and annual service
- **Handyman:** General repairs and maintenance
- **Appliance repair:** As needed

### Vendor Management Tasks
- **Annual:** Review and update vendor contact list
- **Ongoing:** Track service history, costs, and performance
- **Backup:** Maintain 2-3 options for each service type

## Maintenance Cost Benchmarks

### Annual Maintenance Budget (as % of revenue)
- **Rule of thumb:** 10-15% of gross rental income
- **Older properties:** 15-20%
- **Newer properties:** 5-10%

### Typical Annual Costs (Single Property)
| Category | Estimated Annual Cost |
|----------|----------------------|
| HVAC Service | $300-600 |
| Appliance Maintenance | $200-400 |
| Plumbing/Electrical | $300-500 |
| Exterior Maintenance | $500-1,000 |
| Deep Cleaning | $400-800 |
| Safety Equipment | $100-200 |
| Pest Control | $300-600 |
| Landscaping | $500-1,500 |
| **Total** | **$2,600-5,600** |

## Key Insights for System Design

1. **Frequency Matters:** Tasks range from daily (cleaning) to every 7 years (detector replacement) - the system must handle varied schedules

2. **Seasonal Variation:** Many tasks are season-dependent; the system should auto-rotate checklists based on time of year

3. **Professional vs. DIY:** Clear distinction between owner-performed tasks and those requiring licensed professionals

4. **Compliance Critical:** Safety and regulatory tasks cannot be skipped - system should prioritize and flag these

5. **Documentation Essential:** Photo documentation, service records, and cost tracking are valuable for tax and insurance purposes

6. **Vendor Relationships:** Tracking multiple service providers per property adds complexity but is essential

7. **Scale Considerations:** Multi-property owners need aggregated views and efficient batch scheduling
