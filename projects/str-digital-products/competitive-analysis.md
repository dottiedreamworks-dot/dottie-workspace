# STR Digital Products - Competitive Analysis
**Date:** March 8, 2026  
**Purpose:** Research competition before building products

---

## Executive Summary

The STR digital product market is **heavily saturated** on Etsy and other platforms. Most products are basic templates without automation or intelligence. Our opportunity lies in **differentiation through automation, intelligence, and professional design** - not just creating another template.

**Key Finding:** The market has plenty of "checklists" but lacks **smart systems** that actually help hosts make decisions and save time.

---

## 1. PRICING CALCULATOR ($79 Product)

### Direct Competition

**Automated Pricing Tools (SaaS):**
- **PriceLabs** - $19.99-$74.99/month (most popular)
- **Beyond Pricing** - 1% of gross bookings (performance-based)
- **Wheelhouse** - Free tier + $15.99/property/month
- **AirDNA Smart Rates** - Included with market data subscription
- **DPGO** - $18/property/month

**Etsy/Template Competitors:**
- **Short-Term Rental Analysis Calculator** (Etsy) - $15-25
  - Excel/Google Sheets with ROI calculations
  - Static formulas, no dynamic pricing
  - 50+ reviews, basic design
  
- **Real Estate STR Property Setup Calculator** (Etsy) - $12-18
  - Investment analysis focused
  - Tracks budget vs actuals
  - Room-by-room inventory lists
  
- **BNBCalc.com** (Free tool)
  - Address-based revenue prediction
  - STR vs LTR comparison
  - Tax deduction calculator
  - **This is actually sophisticated competition**

- **Stessa** (Free)
  - Rental property financial tracking
  - Cash flow analysis
  - Automated categorization
  - **Strong free alternative**

- **Rabbu** (Free spreadsheet)
  - Airbnb calculator template
  - Investment analysis
  - Market data integration

### Market Reality
**🚨 RED FLAG:** The automated pricing tool space is dominated by well-funded SaaS companies (PriceLabs, Beyond, Wheelhouse). They offer:
- Real-time market data
- Algorithmic price adjustments
- Multi-platform integration
- Professional analytics

**Our $79 calculator cannot compete with $20/month tools that actually adjust prices automatically.**

### Differentiation Opportunity
**Pivot Idea:** Instead of a "pricing calculator," create a:
- **"Direct Booking vs OTA Commission Calculator"** - Shows actual cost of Airbnb/VRBO fees
- **"Pricing Strategy Decision Matrix"** - Helps hosts choose between tools
- **"Rate Optimization Workbook"** - Educational + calculation hybrid

- **"Jurisdiction-Specific House Rules"** - State/county legal requirements
- **"Liability-Protection Rule Sets"** - Lawyer-reviewed language

## Strategic Recommendations

### Products to AVOID (Too Saturated)
1. ❌ **Pricing Calculator** - Can't compete with PriceLabs/Beyond
2. ❌ **Cleaning Checklist** - Commoditized on Etsy
3. ❌ **Basic Welcome Book** - 50+ competitors
4. ❌ **Generic Message Templates** - Widely available for $15
5. ❌ **Simple Expense Tracker** - Free alternatives exist

### Products with POTENTIAL (Differentiation Required)
1. ✅ **House Rules by Jurisdiction** - Underserved, legal value
2. ✅ **Smart Maintenance System** - Automation, not just checklists
3. ✅ **Direct Booking Growth System** - Strategy + templates bundle

### Pivot Strategy
Instead of 6 individual products, create **2 differentiated bundles:**

**Bundle 1: "STR Legal Protection Pack" ($149)**
- Jurisdiction-specific house rules
- Liability waivers
- Insurance documentation templates
- Legal requirement checklists by state

**Bundle 2: "STR Automation System" ($249)**
- Smart maintenance tracking (not checklists)
- Automated guest communication workflows
- Direct booking conversion toolkit
- Revenue optimization analyzer

### Key Differentiators to Pursue
1. **Automation** - Not static templates, but systems that work
2. **Intelligence** - Data-driven recommendations
3. **Legal Protection** - Lawyer-reviewed, jurisdiction-specific
4. **Integration** - Works with existing tools (Airbnb API, PMS)
5. **Support** - Not just templates, but implementation help

---

## Conclusion

**The brutal truth:** Our original 6-product lineup would fail in this market. Etsy is flooded with basic templates, and SaaS tools dominate automation.

**The opportunity:** Build **intelligent systems** that do the work, not just templates that sit in a folder. Focus on:
- Legal protection (high value, low competition)
- Automation (saves time, justifies higher price)
- Strategy (education + tools, not just tools)

**Next Step:** Before building anything, validate demand for the 2-bundle pivot strategy with actual STR hosts.
