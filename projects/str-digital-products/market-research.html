🔧 Product 1: Comprehensive Maintenance Tracking System

The Problem

STR owners struggle with:

  • Remembering when HVAC filters were last changed
  • Tracking appliance warranty expiration dates
  • Coordinating seasonal maintenance (winterization, spring prep)
  • Managing multiple vendors and contractors
  • Documenting maintenance for insurance/tax purposes
  • Preventing costly emergency repairs through proactive maintenance

The Solution: Smart Maintenance Hub

Core Features:

  • Master Task Database: 100+ pre-loaded maintenance tasks specific to STRs
  • Smart Scheduling: Auto-generates calendar based on property specs (age, location, climate)
  • Vendor Management: Store contact info, rates, and history for each service provider
  • Cost Tracking: Budget forecasting and expense logging by category
  • Photo Documentation: Before/after photos with date stamps
  • Multi-Property Support: Dashboard view for portfolio managers
  • Mobile-First: Field-friendly interface for on-site updates
  • Automated Reminders: Email/SMS alerts 7 days, 3 days, and day-of
  • Seasonal Checklists: Auto-rotates based on local climate
  • Compliance Tracking: Permit renewals, inspection dates, safety certifications

📈 Product 2: Direct Booking Growth System

The Problem

STR owners know they should get more direct bookings but:

  • Don't know where to start
  • Think they need expensive website developers
  • Worry about payment processing complexity
  • Don't understand marketing funnels
  • Fear guest screening without platform protection
  • Lack time to figure it all out

The Solution: Direct Booking Blueprint

Core Components:

Module 1: Website Setup (No-Code)

  • Step-by-step guide to building a booking website
  • Template options: WordPress + WooCommerce, Wix, Squarespace
  • Recommended booking engine integrations (Lodgify, OwnerRez)
  • Payment processor setup (Stripe, Square)
  • SSL, domain, hosting recommendations

Module 2: Guest Acquisition

  • Social media strategy for STRs (Instagram, Facebook)
  • Google Business Profile optimization
  • Email marketing setup and automation
  • Content marketing (blogging for local SEO)
  • Referral program templates

Module 3: Guest Conversion

  • Website copy templates (homepage, about, booking page)
  • Professional photography guide
  • Virtual tour creation
  • Trust signals and social proof setup
  • Direct booking incentive strategies

Module 4: Operations & Protection

  • Guest screening framework
  • Damage protection strategies
  • Insurance considerations
  • Automated messaging sequences
  • Review generation system

Delivery Format

Recommended: Comprehensive digital course + toolkit

  • Video lessons (10-15 modules, 5-10 min each)
  • Downloadable templates and checklists
  • Website copy templates (Google Docs)
  • Email sequence templates
  • Bonus: 30-day implementation roadmap
Course Format High Value
Price Point: $97-197 (course + templates). Position as "save $1,000s in OTA fees with one-time investment."
