## Market Analysis Summary

### Saturation Level: MEDIUM-HIGH
There are established players in the ADHD entrepreneurship space, indicating demand but requiring differentiation.

### Key Patterns:
1. **Most channels mix** ADHD general advice with entrepreneurship
2. **Few focus purely** on building businesses with ADHD
3. **Authority positioning** varies: coaches, PhDs, CEOs, doctors
4. **Tone varies:** Clinical → Coaching → Peer support

### Differentiation Opportunities for Jayna:

#### 1. The "Real Business" Angle
- Jayna has ACTUALLY built a 6-figure cabin business
- Most channels are coaches/consultants teaching theory
- Jayna teaches from "in the trenches" experience
- **Unique:** ADHD + Short-term rental business SPECIFICALLY

#### 2. The "Systems Over Willpower" Focus
- Jayna's strength: Creating systems that compensate for ADHD
- Many channels focus on "try harder" or "medication" approaches
- **Unique:** "I built systems because I can't rely on consistency"

#### 3. The "Military Spouse" Angle
- Remote business management
- Building income while PCSing
- Managing business + family + ADHD
- **Unique:** No other channel has this specific life situation

#### 4. Content Format Gap
- Most channels are traditional talking-head videos
- **Opportunity:** Faceless audio content with visuals/screenshots
- Less production pressure on Jayna
- Focus on information density over personality performance
