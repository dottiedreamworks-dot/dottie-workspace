🎢 THRILL RIDE STRATEGY (Priority #1!)

🎢 The Big 3 Coasters at Magic Kingdom

Your daughters are daredevils - these are the MOST INTENSE rides Magic Kingdom has to offer!

  • 1. Seven Dwarfs Mine Train - Family-friendly BUT still thrilling, smooth, 38" height req
    Strategy: Rope drop this FIRST or buy Lightning Lane Single Pass (~$12-15). This will be their warm-up coaster!
  • 2. Space Mountain - Indoor dark coaster, FAST and thrilling, 44" height req
    Strategy: Ride during Early Entry or use Lightning Lane. This is the most intense coaster at Magic Kingdom!
  • 3. Big Thunder Mountain Railroad - Outdoor mine train, FAST with sharp turns, 40" height req
    Strategy: Better at night! Ride during evening for cooler temps and shorter waits. Ride it TWICE if lines allow!
🎢 DAREDEVIL BONUS

If your daughters want EVEN MORE thrills after the Big 3, consider adding a day at Hollywood Studios for:

  • Rock 'n' Roller Coaster - 0-60 mph in 2.8 seconds, loops, inversions!

💡 Budget Tips & Money-Saving Hacks

  • Bring your own snacks and water bottles - Disney allows outside food (no glass, no large coolers)
  • Share meals - Disney portions are huge. Two kids can easily share one kids meal.
  • Free water - Any quick-service restaurant will give you free cups of ice water
  • Skip the Lightning Lane - If budget is tight, you can do this itinerary without it using rope drop strategy
  • Photopass hack - Photopass photographers will take photos with YOUR phone for free
  • Shop off-site - Buy Disney souvenirs at Disney Store or online before/after trip (cheaper)
  • Character dining - Skip it (expensive). Meet characters in the park for free instead
  • Parking - If staying off-site, parking is $30/day at Magic Kingdom
  • Memory Maker - Skip it ($185). Use Photopass photographers with your own phone
  • Breakfast - Eat at hotel or bring granola bars. Don't buy in park
